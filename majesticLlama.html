Let's Explore

Mature llamas weigh an average of 280-350 pounds. Full body size is reached by the fourth year.
"Animal Corner | Animal Facts, Information and Pictures." Animal Corner. N.p., n.d. Web. 11 Sept. 2016.

The llama (Lama glama) is a large camelid that originated in North America about 40 million years ago.

Llama ears are rather long and slightly curved inward, characteristically known as banana shaped.

The height of a full-grown, full-size llama is between 5.5 feet (1.6 metres) to 6 feet (1.8 metres) tall at the top of the head.

The Llama is a very intelligent animal and very easy to train. In just one to five repetitions they will learn and remember many skills.
